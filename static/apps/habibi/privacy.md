# Privacy Policy

**Habibi**
J&S toy2life GmbH
Last updated: January 2026

## Overview

Habibi is a private couples app. Your data stays between you and your partner.

## Data We Collect

**Account Data**
- A unique pairing code to connect you with your partner
- Display name (optional, only visible to your partner)

**Content You Create**
- Notes, drawings, and images you send to your partner
- Daily prompt responses
- Pokes and reactions

## How Data Is Stored

All data is stored in Apple's iCloud (CloudKit) under your Apple ID. We do not operate our own servers. Your content syncs directly between you and your paired partner through Apple's infrastructure.

## Data We Don't Collect

- No analytics or tracking
- No advertising identifiers
- No third-party services
- No crash reporting beyond Apple's standard reports

## Data Sharing

Your content is only shared with your paired partner. We cannot access, read, or sell your data.

## Data Deletion

Unpair from your partner in the app to remove shared data. Delete the app to remove all local data. iCloud data can be managed through your Apple ID settings.

## Children's Privacy

Habibi is not intended for children under 13.

## Changes

We may update this policy and will note the date above.
