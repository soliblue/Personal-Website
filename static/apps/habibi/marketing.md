# Habibi

A private space for you and your loved one.

## Stay Connected

Send handwritten notes, answer daily prompts together, and share little moments throughout the day. Whether you're across the room or across the world, Habibi keeps your connection close.

## Features

**Notes**
Draw and write personal notes with Apple Pencil or your finger. Add stickers and send directly to your partner.

**Daily Prompts**
A new question every day. Answer it, then see what your partner said. Learn something new about each other.

**Pokes**
Send a quick "thinking of you" with a single tap. No words needed.

**Reactions**
React to notes and prompts to let your partner know you saw it.

**Truth or Bluff**
A fun guessing game. How well do you really know each other?

**Memories**
Get "On This Day" reminders of notes from the past.

**Widgets**
See your partner's latest note right from your home screen.

## Privacy First

Your data stays between you and your partner. No ads, no tracking, no third-party access. Everything syncs through your iCloud account.

## Requirements

- iOS 17.0 or later
- iCloud account
- A partner with Habibi installed

## Free

Habibi is completely free. No subscriptions, no in-app purchases.
