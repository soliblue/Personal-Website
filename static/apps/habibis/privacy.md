# Privacy Policy

**Last updated:** January 2026

Habibis is designed with your privacy in mind. We don't track you, we don't sell your data, and we don't want to know your business.

## What We Access

**Contacts (with your permission)**
We access your contacts only to help you add friends to the app. We never upload your contact list to any server.

**iCloud**

## What We Don't Do

- No analytics or tracking
- No advertising
- No third-party data sharing

## Data Storage

All your data lives on your device and in your personal iCloud. Delete the app, and it's gone.
